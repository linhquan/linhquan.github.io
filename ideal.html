Ideal Evolved

Web Design, UI/UX

At Ideal Evolved, a digital agency, I designed websites and newsletters for different clients. Through Ideal Evolved, I learned a lot about web design best practices, designing new content based on a pre-existing visual style and how to use different programs such as Magento, MailChimp, and Wordpress. I created home pages, product pages, navigation menus, about us pages, and landing pages. Some clients needed a website designed from scratch, while other had existing websites that needed pages added on to it.


This client already had an existing website and wanted to create a landing page to enhance their customer’s shopping experience. I designed a landing page for a client’s “Shop By Shape” page to make the customer’s shopping experience easier. In the original webpage, the categories were stacked on top of the product listings.

The agency needed a new page designed for their customized Magento app. This page allowed their clients to download and remove apps to their webpage. The page also needed to be easily sortable and easy to understand.

Some clients had newsletters that Ideal Evolved create. Between website designs, I created e-blasts for their clients. Although I follow a predetermined format, I made sure the layout of the type and image were consistent.
