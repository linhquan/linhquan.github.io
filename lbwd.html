Long Beach Web Design

Web Design, UI/UX

At Long Beach Web Design, I designed websites for many clients of different industries. My main task here was to design new websites for clients. I mainly redesigned the homepage, while some clients requested interior pages to be redesigned as well.


For this client, I redesigned their very outdated website. I worked on two sites for their businesses, one for their real estate firm and one for their property management business. After my initial design, they requested a different menu redesign that emulated their original menu from their property management site.

For the GMEP Engineers site, they requested to combine several pages to add into their home page. After my initial design, they wanted more interactivity to their site, which led to the final design.
