InPrint Magazine

Layout Design, Visual Identity, Publication

InPrint is a design magazine created by a student organization at University of California, Davis. As the Art Director of the magazine, I worked under the co-directors and collaborated with other team leaders to create a magazine. I was responsible for creating marketing materials, creating the graphic standard for the magazine with one of the Co-Directors, Timothea Wang, and overseeing a team of designers to design the magazine.

This is InPrint’s head web designer, photographer, 3 co-directors, art director, and editor-in-chief.

As the Art Director, I led the graphics team and made sure we stay on track and complete the designs in time to send to the printer. Throughout the year, we worked on creating a cohesive first edition, centering around the theme “First Impressions.” The team worked together to decide a color scheme, graphic standard and layout designs. During the InPrint meetings, I directed critiques of work that was brought in for the magazine.
