Meat Munchies

Packaging, Visual Identity

Rou sung (肉鬆) is a cooked and finely shredded meat product with a fluffy and crunchy texture. The product is affordable, convenient to prepare, and can be eaten in many different ways. Currently, rou sung is sold in a limited amount of super market (usually in supermarkets specializing in Asian groceries and goods). To bring awareness to the product, I created Meat Munchies, a brand that aims to create contemporary visuals to attract people of many different cultural backgrounds. Through the creative process, I created a brand, packaging, marketing materials, and a business system for rou sung. You can view my process manual at the end of this page.

Inspired by the molecular structure of the nacre, the inside layer of a nautilus shell, I created a geometric package design. The package is designed to fit and stack together, in order to save space during transportation. The shape also allows for the package to withstand a lot of pressure.
