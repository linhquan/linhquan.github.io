#itsaight Campaign

Message Campaign, Photography, Visual Identity

#itsaight is a message campaign that aims to address the anxiety of growing up and inspire millennials to explore and pursue their passions. I collaborated with Aliana Garcia and Linda Tran to research and design the campaign in the course of 10 weeks. In the end of the campaign, we created a consistent brand, proposal, process manual, business system, marketing materials, website design, and budget.
